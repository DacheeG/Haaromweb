Waldaan Aksiyoona Biizinasii Garee Haarom barattootaa fi dargaggoota ciccimoon qorannoo maaltu naannoo keenya jira? Haala jiru irra dhaabbannee kana booda sochii akkamii gochuu dandeenya? jedhan irratti marii baldhaa erga gaggeessen booda kennaa addaa fi mul’ata nama hunda keessa jiru walitti fidee hojiitti hiikuuf waldaan aksiyoona biizinasii garee haarom hundeeffame. Qabeenyaa fi humna naannoo isaa jiru fayyadamee abbootii aksiyoonaa fi hawaasa isaa fayyaduuf kan dhaabbate waldaan Aksiyoona Biizinasii Garee Haarom dhugoomsituu dheebuu dhalootaati.
